Store Policies

Shipping Policy

We strive to ensure that your order is shipped quickly and efficiently. Please review our shipping policy below:

  • Shipping Time: Orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Delivery typically takes 5-7 business days within the country.
  • Shipping Fees: Free shipping for orders over $50; otherwise, a $5 standard fee applies.
  • International Shipping: Available with additional charges; delivery time may vary.

Return & Refund Policy

We want you to be fully satisfied with your purchase. Please review our return and refund policy:

  • Return Timeframe: Products can be returned within 30 days of purchase.
  • Condition of Products: Must be unused, unopened, and in original packaging.
  • Return Process: Contact customer service via the "Contact Us" page; return shipping is free once approved.
  • Refund Process: Refunds are processed within 7 business days after receiving the returned product.

Exchanges

Interested in exchanging a product? Please review the exchange guidelines:

  • Exchange Timeframe: Requests must be made within 30 days of purchase.
  • Eligibility for Exchange: The product must be unopened, unused, and in its original packaging.
  • Exchange Process: Contact our customer support team to assist with the exchange process.
