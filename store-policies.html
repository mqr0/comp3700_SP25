Store Policies

Shipping Policy

We strive to ensure that your order is shipped quickly and efficiently. Please review our shipping policy below:

  • Shipping Time: Orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Delivery times vary based on location, but typically take 5-7 business days within the country.
  • Shipping Fees: Shipping is free for orders over $50. For orders below $50, a standard shipping fee of $5 applies.
  • International Shipping: We offer international shipping at an additional cost. Delivery times may vary based on location.

Return & Refund Policy

We want you to be fully satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you're not happy with your product, please refer to the following return policy:

  • Return Timeframe: Products can be returned within 30 days from the date of purchase.
  • Condition of Products: The product must be unused, unopened, and in its original packaging.
  • Return Process: To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team via the "Contact Us" page. Once approved, return shipping will be provided at no extra cost.
  • Refund Process: Once we receive the returned product, we will process your refund within 7 business days to your original payment method.

Exchanges

If you'd like to exchange a product, please refer to the following:

  • Exchange Timeframe: Exchanges must be requested within 30 days of purchase.
  • Eligibility for Exchange: The product must be unopened, unused, and in its original packaging.
  • Exchange Process: Please contact our customer support team for assistance with the exchange process.
