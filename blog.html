How to Choose Your Perfume According to the Seasons

Posted on April 1, 2025

Choosing the right perfume for each season can elevate your experience. Here is a guide to help you pick a fragrance that matches the season:

Spring: Floral and fresh scents work well in spring, with light notes of rose, jasmine, and citrus.

Summer: Opt for refreshing, citrusy or aquatic scents like mint, lemon, and sea breeze.

Fall: Warm, spicy notes like cinnamon, vanilla, and amber are perfect for autumn.

Winter: Go for deep and woody perfumes with notes like sandalwood, musk, and tobacco.
